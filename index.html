about us

We design and make one of a kind jewelry pieces.
We are committed to using ethical,
conflict-free and sustainable supply chains to create the jewelry you love.
We do appraisals and remold old ones.
We’re also into importing and exporting gemstones,
metals (gold), diamonds and the materials used in our designs
are compliant to the best practices under the Signet Responsible Sourcing Protocol.
We strive to offer our clients the best genuine and
quality products. We’re also experts in valuing diamonds
with GIA certificates, or certificates
from IGI, AGS, HRD or EGL. We have the best GIA Diamond Graduate gemologist on staff.
